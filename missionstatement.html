The Front Page
Have the courage to use your own understanding.

The purpose of The Front Page is to provide the reader with:


  • The best of journalism from around the web.

  • Historical and current context on the main stories of the day - we will assume intelligence from the reader, not knowledge.

  • The best arguments of the different views of the story - we will not, however, provide balance for balance’s sake - sometimes there aren’t two sides to a story, sometimes there are more than two.

  • Stories that are important but aren’t necessarily current, for example, development rates in the third world.

  • The stories that the reader isn’t seeing - whether this be on a local level or on a global level.

  • The information that they need when they vote - we will not be leading with stories about a politician stumbling over their words or what clothes they are wearing.

  • Stories that could threaten the stability of the life of the reader - this ranges from stories about climate inaction to stories about the economy.

  • Stories about what your government is doing, and not doing - this won’t be interesting a lot of the time - but it will be important.

  • A promise that we will get things wrong every now and again - but we will apologise and correct ourselves when we do.

What we are

A start-up news site focused on delivering the facts and the best in journalism from around the web.
