10 dead following clashes on Pakistan-India border.

    The Facts Today:

  • At least 10 are dead following clashes between Pakistani and Indian troops in the border region of Kashmir.

  • A spokesperson for the Indian army accused soldiers from Pakistan of an ‘unprovoked’ attack at a border post.

  • The Pakistani military have said that Indian troops committed ‘unprovoked cease-fire violations’.

  • Pakistan have said 5 civilians and 1 soldiers have died. India have said 2 soldiers and 1 civilian has died.

  • Tensions between the nations(both of which have nuclear weapons) have risen since India revoked the autonomy of the Kashmir region on August 5.

  • Kashmir is a region in the north of India, that is also part of Pakistan and China.
